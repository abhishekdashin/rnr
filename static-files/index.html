Step 1: Submit Your Work

This is your opportunity to share work you're proud of. We've streamlined the process to make it as simple as possible. Submissions should focus on completed work, effective problem-solving, and process improvements that have made a difference.

What to Include:
  • Project/Task Title & Team Members
  • Brief Description & Problem Solved
  • Quantifiable Impact and Outcomes
  • "Before and After" for Process Improvements
  • Supporting Materials (Links to docs, demos)
How to Submit:

Use the standardized template (form, Confluence page, etc.) and submit it through our dedicated channel (e.g., a specific Slack channel or shared drive).

What is "Good Work"?

Think about work that reduced latency, cut costs, improved a workflow, or solved a tricky problem, even if it was escalated. If it made a positive impact, we want to hear about it!

Step 2: Review & Feedback

Every submission is carefully reviewed to understand its contributions. The goal is to provide constructive feedback to all submitters and identify work that has had a significant impact. Reviews will be conducted regularly to ensure timeliness.

Review Team:

Initially, Abhishek and Shabab will conduct reviews. We plan to rotate in other team members to foster shared ownership and diverse perspectives.

Review Criteria:
  • Impact: Contribution to team/organization goals.
  • Quality: Technical excellence and thoroughness.
  • Efficiency: Improvements in processes or resources.
  • Problem-Solving: Effectiveness of the solution.
  • Clarity: Quality of the submission itself.

Step 3: Leadership Showcase

The most impactful work selected during the review process will be presented to the leadership group each quarter. This is a fantastic platform to gain visibility and showcase the value our team delivers to the entire organization.

Presentation Format:

Presenters will receive a template to ensure presentations are consistent, clear, and impactful. Key elements include the problem statement, solution, key results, and lessons learned.

Presentation Coaching:

We want you to succeed. Presenters will have the opportunity to rehearse and receive coaching and feedback before the main event to help build confidence and refine their message.

Step 4: Celebrate & Recognize

Recognition is at the heart of this framework. We believe in celebrating success publicly and providing opportunities that support professional growth. This is about acknowledging the hard work and ingenuity of our team members.
